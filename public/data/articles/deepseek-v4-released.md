DeepSeek released V4 in early March 2026, a roughly 1-trillion parameter Mixture-of-Experts model that activates approximately 37 billion parameters per token — maintaining the same efficiency ratio as V3 but dramatically scaled. The model introduces a conditional memory mechanism called Engram, enabling a 1-million token context window.

Leaked benchmark results claim 90% on HumanEval and over 80% on SWE-bench Verified for software engineering tasks, placing V4 among the top-performing models globally. Combined with pricing approximately 4.5× lower than comparable Western models, V4 is accelerating the already rapid commoditisation of frontier-level intelligence.

The release continues the pattern established by DeepSeek's earlier models: high capability at low inference cost, built with a lean training approach that challenges the assumption that frontier AI requires massive capital expenditure. DeepSeek V4 is already being adopted in enterprise workflows where reasoning quality directly affects output value — mathematical pipelines, multi-step agents, and structured data extraction.
